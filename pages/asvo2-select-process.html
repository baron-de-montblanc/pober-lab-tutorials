Downloading MWA Data from ASVO

Selecting and Processing Data

Clicking on the "OBSERVATIONS" button at the top of the screen takes you to the observations page which lists every single available MWA observation:

On the left, there are a bunch of filters allowing you to select observations based on the date, the observation ID (OBSID), the pointing, and several other options. Additionally, clicking on the little "i" icon next to any OBSID takes you to a separate page containing all the relevant information pertaining to that observation (for example):

Once you have found the observation you would like to download, simply click on its OBSID. This should take you to the job page:

In almost all cases, "Visibility Conversion Job" is what you want. This job type converts the data from the raw MWA correlator format to either the CASA Measurement Set or to UVFITS format. You have options to set the time resolution, frequency resolution, apply calibration, etc. Not setting anything defaults to the true observational parameters.

Once you are satisfied with the parameters you selected, hit "Submit". To view the status of your job, click the big "MY JOBS" button at the top of the screen.

It may take a bit of time to go through the queue. After "Queued", your job should say "Processing". Once it's done processing, it'll say "Completed" and a green download button will appear:

Cliking on this download button will initiate the download to your own computer, which you don't generally want to do. To download it directly to Oscar, you will need to use the MWA ASVO client giant-squid. Move on to the next section for instructions on how to install and use this client!
